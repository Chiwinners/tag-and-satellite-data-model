NOTES (shared across datasets)

• Keys: (lat, lon, time_bin) must be consistently defined (same binning anchor and spatial resolution).
• Units: confirm dSST_raw units (°C/km vs grid units) and EKE source scale; CHL in mg m⁻³.
• Normalization: z-scores typically computed per region/season to improve spatiotemporal robustness.
• Effort: can also be used as sample weights or log-offset during training.
• MAXENT prior: may be used as a feature and/or as a logit-prior regularizer in the BINN.
